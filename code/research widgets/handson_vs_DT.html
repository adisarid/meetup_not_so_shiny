The screen compares two methods of rendring tabular data: DataTable versus handsontable. Some pointers:

  • DataTable is quick with a lot of data, works server or client side, and has tons of great features easily built into it. Its editing capabilities are limited.
  • rhandsontable is slow but much more flexible in terms of editing values, generating validation rules while editing (like a small list box), checkboxes in TRUE/FALSE columns, and more.
  • DataTable seems to be more common than rhandsontable (around 630k versus 15k downloads per month, according to cranlog statistics).
